About Me

Hello and welcome to The Website I have created for the Web Design and User Interface for the HDip in Computing in DIT.

My name is Gavin McCoy and I am born and bred in Newbridge, County Kildare, Ireland.

My background is originally in Chemistry. I graduated from Dublin City University with a BSc in Chemical and Pharmaceutical Sciences. After graduating, I worked in Pfizer Grange Castle for over two years in the Quality Control department.

Working there, I gained a huge range of skills. It enabled me to be a fantastic team player by helping others with any problems they may have faced as well participating in whiteboard meetings. I know that whiteboard meetings are often an integral part of the IT industry and it is something I am quite comfortable with and enjoy taking part in.

While the pharmaceutical industry offer numerous opportunities, I felt that the IT industry offered more and with a natural aptitude for computers, it seemed like a no-brainer to make a career change. I started the HDip in Computing in DIT in February 2015 and I have never looked back. It was definitely the best decision of my life. I have improved my leadership skills through a group assignment for Systems Analysis and Testing where I also learned a great about UML and can now understand any UML diagram you could throw at me as well as doing quite a good job at created my own. I have also studied Databases, Object-orientated Programming, Web Design, Computer Architecture and more.
